A Beginner's Guide to Hanfu

How to explore hanfu through shape, fabric and occasion while keeping comfort and curiosity at the center.

2026-06-10·8 min read

Table of contents

  1. Start with a broad definition
  2. Choose for comfort and context
  3. Let details lead your learning

Start with a broad definition

Hanfu is a modern umbrella term for clothing traditions associated with Han Chinese history. It includes many silhouettes that changed across centuries, rather than one fixed costume.

“汉服”是现代常用的概括性称呼，指与汉族历史相关的服饰传统。它包含多个世纪中不断变化的众多形制，并不是一套固定不变的服装。

A museum label, maker or experienced wearer can help identify a specific form. Beginners do not need to memorize every dynasty before appreciating construction and movement.

博物馆说明、制作者或有经验的穿着者，都能帮助你辨认具体形制。初学者不必先背完所有朝代，也可以欣赏服装的结构与动态。

Choose for comfort and context

For a first try, prioritize a breathable fabric, secure closures and a hem you can walk in safely. Ask the shop how the layers fasten and whether assistance is included.

第一次尝试时，应优先考虑透气面料、牢固系带，以及方便安全行走的衣长。可以询问店家各层如何穿系，是否提供穿衣协助。

A photo studio, cultural event and relaxed park visit have different needs. Check venue rules and weather, and avoid letting long sleeves or accessories obstruct shared paths.

摄影棚、文化活动和轻松逛公园的需求各不相同。提前确认场地规则与天气，并注意不要让长袖或配饰妨碍公共通道。

Let details lead your learning

Notice collar direction, sleeve shape, woven patterns and how a garment creates volume without modern tailoring. Each detail offers a path into textile history and craft.

留意衣领方向、袖形、织纹，以及服装如何在没有现代剪裁的情况下形成体量。每个细节都能带你走进纺织史与工艺。

Buy slowly and ask makers about materials and care. A well-understood, comfortable piece is a better beginning than a large wardrobe chosen in haste.

购买时不妨放慢速度，向制作者询问面料与保养。与其匆忙添置一大柜衣服，不如先从一件真正了解且穿着舒适的衣服开始。
